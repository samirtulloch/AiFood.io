How it works

Your journey to healthy eating in 3 simple steps

01

Tell us what you like (and what not)

Never waste time deciding what to eat again! A weekly meal plan that is 100% customized for you will be made by AiFood's algorithm. Regardless of the diet you choose, it guarantees that you get all the vitamins and minerals you require!

02

Approve the weekly meal plan

Approve the weekly meal plan that AiFood's AI has created for you. You may add your own flare to the recipes, switch out entire meals, or just individual ingredients.

03

Choose a time convenient for you

Every day, our team of top chefs in the area will prepare the meal of your choice, and we will deliver it straight to your door at a time that suits you. The delivery time and address are subject to daily change!
